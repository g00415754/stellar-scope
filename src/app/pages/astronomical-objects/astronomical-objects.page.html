Get the Most Out of Your Skywatching Experience

While you explore the night sky with Stellarium, here are a few tips to help you with real-life stargazing:

  • Find a Dark Location: The further you are from city lights, the clearer the sky will be.
  • Use a Red Light: If you need to read a map or adjust your equipment at night, use a red flashlight to preserve your night vision.
  • Take Your Time: Stargazing is about patience. Take your time to observe the objects you see and try to connect the dots between constellations.
